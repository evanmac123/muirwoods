Getting Started

Boys Residential Treatment in Sonoma, CA

Our residential treatment program for boys 12–17, situated on six idyllic acres in Sonoma County, CA, accepts just ten boys at one time. This small number of boys allows for highly individualized and extremely effective treatment for mental health issues such as trauma, depression, or poor self esteem that often lead to self-medication with drugs and/or alcohol. In this setting, where boys are valued and engaged, healing can and does occur.

Boys & Girls Outpatient Program in Marin, CA

Our outpatient program, located in Marin County, CA, serves teen boys and girls 12–19 with behavioral and mental health, and substance use issues. Our highly credentialed clinical team works closely with teens and their families to develop individualized treatment plans that may include group therapy, cognitive behavior therapy, dialectal behavior therapy, and when needed, medication management and neuropsychological testing.

Meet Our Renowned and Highly Credentialed Treatment Team

Leading our staff of medical and clinical professionals are two of the industry’s best-known experts on the treatment of adolescent mental health, behavioral, and substance use issues. David E. Smith, M.D., serves as our chair of Addiction Medicine and is recognized as a national leader in the treatment of adolescent substance abuse, the psychopharmacology of drugs, and new research strategies in the management of drug abuse problems. Clinical Director Jennifer Golick, Ph.D., LMFT, specializes in cognitive behavioral therapy and motivational interviewing. Her focus is on helping adolescents and their families identify problematic patterns of thinking in order to affect long term systemic change.
